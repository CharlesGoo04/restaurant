Our Story

A passion for culinary excellence since 1995

A Legacy of Excellence

Founded in 1995 by renowned chef Jean-Pierre Dubois, Maison has been at the forefront of culinary innovation for over two decades. Our journey began with a simple yet powerful vision: to create extraordinary dining experiences that celebrate the artistry of fine cuisine.

Today, under the guidance of Executive Chef Marie Laurent, we continue to push boundaries while honoring traditional techniques and the finest seasonal ingredients.
